💳 Payment & Licence
What do I get with each plan?
  • Base — ₹450 (~$5.40): 6 months of full core access — job search across 40+ boards, one-click autofill, interview prep, hackathon links, application tracker, salary benchmarks. No AI resume tailoring.
  • Bundle — ₹750 (~$9.00): Everything in Base plus 50 AI resume tailors. Best value for active job seekers.
  • AI Credits top-up: ₹350 / 50 tailors · ₹600 / 100 tailors · ₹1000 / 200 tailors. Requires Base access. Credits never expire.
Base access is valid for 6 months from purchase. After expiry, renew at ₹450. AI credits are separate and never expire.
Where is my licence key?
Your licence key was emailed to the address you provided at checkout. It looks like JHG-XXXX-XXXX-XXXX. Check your spam/promotions folder if you don't see it within 5 minutes of payment. If you still can't find it, email us with your Razorpay payment ID.
How do I activate my licence key?
  1. Open the JobHunter Global Chrome extension
  2. On the welcome / paywall screen, click "Already paid? Restore purchases →"
  3. Enter your licence key in the format JHG-XXXX-XXXX-XXXX
  4. Click Activate →
The extension will verify your key and unlock access immediately. You can also use the restore tool at the top of this page.

Your licence key works across reinstalls and devices. Simply enter it again using the restore process above (or the tool at the top of this page). Your key is permanent — the only thing that counts is whether 6 months have passed since purchase.
My Base access expired — what happens to my AI credits?
AI credits never expire and are stored separately. If your 6-month Base access lapses, renew at ₹450 — your existing AI credits are restored immediately once Base is active again.

Do you offer a free trial?
There's no time-limited trial, but you can download and install the extension free from the Chrome Web Store to explore the interface before purchasing. All features require a paid licence to use.
⚡ Features & How-To
How do I use the AI resume tailoring?
  1. Go to the Resume tab in the extension
  2. Upload your existing resume (PDF, DOCX, or TXT — max 200 KB) or rely on your saved profile
  3. Paste the job description you're targeting
  4. Either add your Anthropic API key in Profile tab (free to get at console.anthropic.com), or purchase AI Credits
  5. Click "Tailor Resume as Hiring Manager"
You'll get a 0–100 match score, honest hiring manager feedback, a rewritten resume with skills grouped by relevance, and 5 likely interview questions.
What is a BYOK (Bring Your Own Key) API key?
Instead of purchasing AI credits, you can use your own Anthropic API key — free to get at console.anthropic.com. Each resume tailor costs roughly $0.01 billed directly to your Anthropic account. Go to Profile tab → AI Access → enter your key (starts with sk-ant-…) and save. The Analyze button will appear in the Resume tab.
Autofill isn't filling my application form — what should I do?
  1. Make sure your profile is saved in the Profile tab (name, email, phone, etc.)
  2. Navigate to the job application page and wait for it to fully load
  3. Click the extension icon → go to Autofill tab → click "Autofill This Page"
  4. Alternatively, look for the floating ⚡ Autofill button at the bottom-right of the page
Autofill works by writing to form fields. Some sites (e.g. LinkedIn Easy Apply multi-step forms) may need you to scroll through pages and autofill each step. Works best on Naukri, Indeed, Lever, Greenhouse, and standard HTML forms.
The search isn't returning results / opening job sites
Each "result" is a link that opens that job site's search page in a new tab — it's not an aggregated results feed. Make sure:
  • At least one site chip is selected (click a chip to toggle it on/off)
  • You've entered a job title in the search field
  • Chrome has permission to open new tabs (allow pop-ups if blocked)
If no tabs open, check Chrome's pop-up blocker settings for the extension.
The prep tab is empty after tailoring my resume
The prep guide populates after a successful AI resume tailor. Once the analysis completes, click "Prep Guide →" in the Resume tab results, or switch to the Prep tab manually. If it's still empty, try re-running the tailor — occasional AI response timeouts can cause incomplete results.
How does the Application Tracker work?
Every time you click "Apply on [Site] ↗" in a search result, the application is automatically logged with the job title, company, date, and listing URL. You can also add applications manually in the Tracker tab → "+ Add Application". Change status (Applied → Interviewing → Offered → Rejected) using the dropdown on each card. All data is stored locally on your device.
How do I switch between countries?
Click the country flag at the top of the Search tab. A dropdown opens with 50+ countries — search by name or scroll. Selecting a new country rebuilds the site chips with the relevant job boards for that region, and resets the selection to All.
🔒 Privacy & Data
Where is my profile data stored?
All your data — name, email, phone, skills, experience, API key, and application history — is stored only on your device using Chrome's local storage API. It never leaves your browser. We have no server that receives or stores your personal data.
What data is sent when I tailor a resume?
When you click "Tailor Resume", your profile data (or uploaded resume text) and the job description are sent directly to Anthropic's API (api.anthropic.com). This is the only external call the extension makes. Your API key is sent as an HTTP header directly to Anthropic — it never passes through our servers. See our Privacy Policy for full details.
How do I delete all my data?
Uninstalling the extension removes all locally stored data. Alternatively: Chrome → Settings → Privacy & Security → Site Settings → View permissions and data stored across sites → search "JobHunter" → Clear data.
