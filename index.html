4-Week Training Plan

Day-by-day prep schedule

Generated from the job description you analyse in the extension.

W1

Technical Foundations

Build core knowledge for the role

Day 1: Review the job description — list every required skill and tool
Day 2: Study the top 2 technical skills from the JD (videos + docs)
Day 3: Build a small project or exercise using those skills
Day 4: Review common system design patterns for this role
Day 5: Take 1 mock test on the role's core technology
W2

Company Deep-Dive

Research the company thoroughly

Day 1: Read the company's About, Mission, and recent news
Day 2: Study their products, tech stack, and competitors
Day 3: Read Glassdoor reviews — find culture signals
Day 4: Prepare your "Why this company?" answer (3 specific reasons)
Day 5: Prepare 5 intelligent questions to ask the interviewer
W3

Interview Practice

Behavioral and technical mock rounds

Day 1: Write STAR answers for 5 behavioral questions
Day 2: Record yourself answering — review for filler words
Day 3: Technical mock interview (use Pramp or interviewing.io)
Day 4: Case study or scenario practice (role-specific)
Day 5: Full mock interview with a friend or mentor
W4

Polish & Simulate

Final prep and confidence building

Day 1: Final resume review — ensure it matches the JD keywords
Day 2: Research salary range — prepare your negotiation number
Day 3: Full simulation — 60-min mock with timer
Day 4: Rest and light review of your notes
Day 5: Interview day — checklist below ✅
Behavioral Questions
BQ 1 — Leadership
Tell me about a time you led a team through a difficult situation.
STAR: Set the scene (2 sentences) → Your specific task → 3 concrete actions you took → Quantified result + lesson
BQ 2 — Failure
Tell me about a time you failed and what you learned.
STAR: Be honest about the failure → Take ownership → Show what you changed → Prove you applied the lesson
BQ 3 — Conflict
Describe a time you disagreed with a colleague or manager.
STAR: Focus on the issue not the person → Show empathy → Explain your approach to resolution → Outcome
BQ 4 — Delivery
Tell me about delivering something under a very tight deadline.
STAR: Set context → Your specific role → Prioritisation decisions → Metric result (time saved, revenue, etc.)
BQ 5 — Data-Driven
Give an example of a decision you made using data.
STAR: What data you had → How you analysed it → Decision made → Measurable outcome
